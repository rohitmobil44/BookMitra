📖 About BookMitra

BookMitra is a student-focused platform designed to make finding and buying books simple, affordable, and convenient.

We help students discover the best books for their studies, especially for Class 9 and Class 10. Our platform connects users with trusted online marketplaces like Flipkart.

Our mission is to support students by saving their time and helping them find the right study materials at the best prices.

We do not directly sell books. We only recommend and redirect users to trusted platforms.
